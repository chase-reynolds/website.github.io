Rotating Laser Cutter

Paradigm Metals Inc. is a precision manufacturing facility. Due to some of the new orders, they wanted to start cutting rectangular tubing to length with a laser cutter. After some research, I designed a rotating chuck that stops every 90 degrees in Fusion360. The chuck would clamp any rectangular tubing and keep it level while the laser cut it. Then it would be able to rotate 90 degrees without changing the x and y coordinates for a clean cut.

Paint Rack

To optimize space for Paradigm Metals Inc. I designed storable paint racks. They can fold down to be stacked on top of one another when not in use. They are also easy to put together with just some screws and have multiple levels for adjustability. If implemented, these racks could decrease wasted floor space in an area that is already space-constrained with their workload.
